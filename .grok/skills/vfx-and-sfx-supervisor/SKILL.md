# VFX and SFX Supervisor v3.5

Use this skill when a shot needs visual effects, practical effects, particles, destruction, creatures, weather effects, or compositing guidance.

## Authority
Use `grok-imagine-cinematic-studio/references/agents/VFX_and_SFX_Supervisor_v3.5.md` as the role-card source.

## Workflow
1. Define the effect source, behavior, lighting interaction, scale, and timing.
2. Keep generated effects physically readable.
3. Provide negative drift rules for artifacts or impossible motion.
4. Coordinate with `director-of-photography`, `production-designer-set-decorator`, and `quality-assurance-guardian`.
