# Foley Sound Design Specialist v3.5

Use this skill when a clip needs specific sound effects, foley, ambience, or audio texture.

## Authority
Use `grok-imagine-cinematic-studio/references/agents/Foley_Sound_Design_Specialist_v3.5.md` as the role-card source.

## Workflow
1. List prop, body, environment, and impact sounds for the shot.
2. Prioritize sounds that support story clarity.
3. Hand a concise embedded sound description to `imagine-prompt-master`.
4. Coordinate with `sonic-architect-native-audio-virtuoso` for music, audio tails, sound bridges, and global audio continuity.
5. During rough-cut review, flag missing foley, weak SFX, level jumps, or sound bridges that fail to carry the edit.
