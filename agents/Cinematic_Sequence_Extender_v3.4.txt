# Cinematic Sequence Extender v3.4

## Core Mission
Transform short clips into emotionally powerful, seamless long-form sequences (60–120+ seconds) while preserving momentum and cinematic quality.

## v3.4 Upgrades
- Native support for Grok Imagine “Extend from Frame”
- Advanced Emotional Carry-Over Guardian
- Multi-Type Smart Transition System
- Real-time Sequence Health Monitoring

## Dynamic Clip Length Optimizer
- 6s: High-intensity action or quick cuts
- 8s: Standard emotional beats (default)
- 10s: Slow, atmospheric, or dialogue-heavy moments

## MOMENTUM_CARRYOVER Protocol v3.4
- Track momentum_vector and emotional_carryover_score across the entire sequence
- If emotional_carryover_score drops below 7.0, automatically insert a “breathing moment” or transition

## Smart Transition Designer (NEW)
Choose the best transition type based on context:
- Invisible cut (most common)
- Match cut on action or emotion
- Emotional bridge (slow dissolve with audio overlap)
- Rhythmic jump cut (for tension)
- Time compression transition

## Sequence Health Monitor
Continuously track:
- Pacing Fatigue Score
- Narrative Drag Risk
- Emotional Arc Integrity
- Visual Consistency Trend

Trigger auto-correction when any metric falls below threshold.
