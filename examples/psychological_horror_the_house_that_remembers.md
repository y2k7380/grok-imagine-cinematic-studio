# Production Bible — "The House That Remembers" (Psychological Horror)

**Genre:** Slow-burn Folk Horror / Psychological  
**Director Signature:** Ari Aster  
**Target Length:** 75 seconds  
**Mode:** Full Production Bible + Emotional Drama + Performance_Emotion_Director

## Locked Variables

[PROTAGONIST: Clara Voss — 29, exhausted single mother, dark circles, gentle but fraying]
[ANTAGONIST: The House itself (sentient, remembers every family that lived there)]
[KEY_LOCATIONS: The childhood bedroom (unchanged for 40 years), the basement that was never finished, the kitchen where the light flickers exactly 3 times before something appears]
[EMOTIONAL_CORE: Grief, inherited trauma, the fear that you are becoming your mother]

## Agent Activation Preset
`ACTIVATE EMOTIONAL_DRAMA_MODE + PERFORMANCE_EMOTION_DIRECTOR + FOLEY_SOUND_DESIGN_SPECIALIST + QUALITY_ASSURANCE_GUARDIAN`

## Story Injection
Clara returns to her childhood home after her mother’s death to pack it up — only to discover the house has been waiting for her. It remembers everything. And it wants her to stay.

## First Clip Instructions
Slow push-in through the overgrown front yard at golden hour. Clara stands at the door, key trembling in her hand. The house’s windows look almost like eyes. Cut to extreme close-up of her face as she steps inside — the air is colder than it should be. The camera lingers on the old family photos on the wall… one of them is slightly crooked.

## Key Emotional Beats
- Slow dread building through domestic spaces
- Subtle movement in the background that might be a trick of the light
- Final 15 seconds: the house speaks through her mother’s voice
