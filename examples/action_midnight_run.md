# Production Bible — "Midnight Run" (High-Octane Action Thriller)

**Genre:** Car Chase / Heist Action  
**Director Signature:** Christopher Nolan (practical-heavy, IMAX scale, time pressure)  
**Target Length:** 85 seconds  
**Mode:** Long Sequence + Maximum Consistency + Stunt_Action_Choreographer + VFX_SFX_Supervisor

## Locked Variables

[PROTAGONIST: Jax Rivera — 32, former getaway driver, now doing one last job for his brother’s freedom]
[ANTAGONIST: Detective Harlan Voss — relentless, by-the-book, personal grudge]
[KEY_SEQUENCE: 4-minute continuous car chase through rainy downtown at 2:17 AM]
[VEHICLES: 1969 Mustang (Jax), black unmarked Charger (Harlan), civilian traffic as moving obstacles]

## Agent Activation Preset
`ACTIVATE MAXIMUM_CONSISTENCY_MODE + STUNT_CHOREOGRAPHER + VFX_SFX_SUPERVISOR + SONIC_ARCHITECT`

## Story Injection
Jax has 47 minutes to deliver a stolen drive across the city before his brother is executed. Every second counts. Every red light is a death sentence.

## First Clip Instructions (EXECUTE FIRST CLIP)
Night. Rain. Jax slams the Mustang into gear and launches from an underground garage. Tires scream. Cut to wide shot of the car exploding onto the wet street. Headlights carve through rain. Harlan’s Charger appears in the rearview mirror two blocks back.

## Key Beats
- Multiple near-misses with civilian cars
- Practical tire blowout + spin
- Final 20 seconds: bridge jump with real physics

**Use with:** Stunt_Action_Choreographer + VFX_SFX_Supervisor for maximum impact.
