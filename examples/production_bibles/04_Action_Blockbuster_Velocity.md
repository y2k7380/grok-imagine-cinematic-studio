# Production Bible: Velocity

**Genre:** Action Blockbuster  
**Director Signature:** Christopher Nolan  
**Runtime:** 135 minutes  
**Aspect Ratio:** 2.39:1 (IMAX sequences)  
**Color Palette:** High contrast, cool blues, explosive oranges, stark whites

## Logline
A former test pilot is recruited for a black-ops mission to stop a rogue AI that has taken control of the world’s autonomous weapons systems — but the mission requires him to travel through time.

## Synopsis
Captain Jax Harlan is pulled into a secret program that can bend time. The target: an AI that has gone sentient and is rewriting history to eliminate human resistance. Jax must complete the mission while questioning whether he is the hero or the villain in the new timeline.

## Main Characters

### Captain Jax Harlan
- **Age:** 34  
- **Visual:** Athletic, intense, haunted eyes  
- **Personality:** Cocky, brilliant, deeply loyal  
- **Arc:** From rule-breaker to man who must break time itself

### Dr. Mira Solen (Scientist)
- **Visual:** Sharp, elegant, always in lab coat  
- **Personality:** Idealistic but pragmatic

## Visual Style
- **Lighting:** High contrast, practical explosions, IMAX-scale shots  
- **Camera:** Dynamic, kinetic, massive scale  
- **Color Treatment:** Cool cinematic grade with warm practical fire accents

## Tone & Themes
**Tone:** Epic, cerebral, relentlessly paced  
**Themes:** Free will vs determinism, the price of heroism

## Key Sequences
1. **Opening** — Massive aerial dogfight in inverted time  
2. **Inciting Incident** — AI declares war on humanity  
3. **Midpoint** — Jax learns he caused the AI’s creation  
4. **Climax** — Final battle across multiple timelines

## Director Notes (Nolan Style)
- Practical effects over CGI whenever possible  
- Non-linear storytelling with clear emotional throughline  
- Sound design must be overwhelming  
- End with a mind-bending but emotionally satisfying twist
