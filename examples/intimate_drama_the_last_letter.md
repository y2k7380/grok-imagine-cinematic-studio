# Production Bible — "The Last Letter" (Intimate Character Drama)

**Genre:** Quiet Emotional Drama / Romance  
**Director Signature:** Roger Deakins (volumetric light, rain, emotional color temperature)  
**Target Length:** 60 seconds  
**Mode:** Full Production Bible + Performance_Emotion_Director + Narrative_Arc_Pacing_Strategist

## Locked Variables

[PROTAGONIST: Elias — 41, quiet bookstore owner, gentle hands, always slightly rumpled]
[LOVE_INTEREST: Lena — 38, dying of a rare illness, fierce and luminous]
[KEY_PROP: A handwritten letter Lena wrote 12 years ago that Elias never sent]
[EMOTIONAL_CORE: Regret, second chances, the beauty and terror of saying everything before it’s too late]

## Agent Activation Preset
`ACTIVATE EMOTIONAL_DRAMA_MODE + PERFORMANCE_EMOTION_DIRECTOR + POST_PRODUCTION_COLOR_GRADING_SUPERVISOR`

## Story Injection
Elias finds the letter he wrote to Lena the night she left. She’s back in town for one week. This is their last chance.

## First Clip Instructions
Soft morning light through rain-streaked windows of the bookstore. Elias stands alone among the shelves, holding the unopened letter. His hands are shaking. Cut to close-up of the letter — the ink has slightly faded. Rain outside creates beautiful god rays across his face.

## Key Emotional Beats
- 00:00 – 00:20: Quiet longing and regret
- 00:21 – 00:40: First meeting in 12 years (tremendous restraint)
- 00:41 – 01:00: The decision to finally speak

**Tone:** Slow cinema. Every glance and silence matters.
