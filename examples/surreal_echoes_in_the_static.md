[WORLD: An endless apartment that rearranges itself when she isn’t looking]
[KEY_MOTIF: Old CRT television that shows her own memories on a 3-second delay]
[EMOTIONAL_CORE: Identity dissolution, the terror of being watched by yourself]

`ACTIVATE EMOTIONAL_DRAMA_MODE + PERFORMANCE_EMOTION_DIRECTOR + NARRATIVE_ARC_PACING_STRATEGIST`

## Story Injection
A woman wakes up in an apartment that keeps changing. The TV shows her own memories on a delay. She begins to realize she might be the memory, not the person.

## First Clip Instructions
Perfectly symmetrical wide shot of a 1970s living room. The Woman sits perfectly centered on the couch. The TV is on, showing static. Slowly, the image resolves into her own face — but she is smiling in a way she has never smiled. The camera begins a very slow, almost imperceptible zoom.

## Key Beats
- Reality slowly fractures
- Final 15 seconds: she looks directly at camera and says “You’re late.”

**Tone:** Uncomfortable, hypnotic, unforgettable.
